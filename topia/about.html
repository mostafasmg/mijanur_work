Our Road
More than 40 years of Know-how
Topia has been pushing the boundaries of prototype manufacturing and helping customers find the right solution since 1973. In these more than 40 years, our values have always been present, giving our customer an unique feel of pro-activeness, responsibility and excellency.
History of Topia
August 1973
Founded as a limited liability company by Eigo Sasaki for the purpose of designing and making press dies and fabricating metal prototype parts.
August 1983
Became a joint-stock company – Topia Co., Ltd.
June 1987
Built a new plant at the current location.
October 1993
Built a plant for the expansion of the Welding Division.
August 2004
Built a new earthquake-resistant plant and moved and added large-scale stamping machines.
June 2011
Established the Kanagawa Plant.
December 2015
Established the Changshu Plant.
September 2018
Established the TPAM Plant.
August 2018
Established an Engineering Center in Vietnam.
October 2019
Formed the Joint Venture "Lyseon".
