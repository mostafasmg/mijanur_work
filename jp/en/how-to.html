HOW TO
DEVELOP A SYSTEM
Flow from order to delivery
HOW TO 01.
Contact Us

Any kind of requirement is accepted. First of all, please feel free to contact us from the mail form on the homepage.
HOW TO 02.
Hearing

Based on the contents of the inquiry, our system engineer will be involved in consulting on the feasibility of systemization and how much it will cost. The person in charge will hear the information necessary for building the system and the flow of the work from the person in charge, and based on that will summarize the overall system concept including the operation.
HOW TO 03.
Proposal And Estimate Presentation

Based on the interviews, we will provide you with a proposal (rough image with screen image, schedule) and the cost of system development.
HOW TO 04.
Order

After a thorough discussion, we will solidify the concept, determine the amount (payment method), delivery date, etc., and will receive a formal order letter.
HOW TO 05.
Requirement Definition

We will propose a system developed for a business that can be improved, summarize the requirements required for the system, and create requirement definition document.
HOW TO 06.
Basic Design

Based on the requirement definition we will create the basic design of the system, screen design, list of functions and form design, etc.
HOW TO 07.
Detailed Design

Based on the basic design we will perform the internal design of the system.
HOW TO 08.
Development Of

We will proceed with the development based on each created design. If a new specification is added during development, we will develop a versatile system to respond to the request. We will assure you that additional functions will be added.
HOW TO 09.
Operational Test

Perform operation tests of the developed system. We will test from various viewpoints from the standpoint of the user, such as operation check test, system processing performance test, and ease of use in operation.
HOW TO 10.
Verification

Final confirmation on the system to be installed.
HOW TO 11.
Delivery

We provide the developed system and the materials and manuals required for operation.
HOW TO 12.
Operation

It supports daily operations such as regular operation such as system operation check and backup, and support in case of trouble.
HOW TO 13.
Maintenance

We will respond to additions and changes of functions after the system is introduced.
