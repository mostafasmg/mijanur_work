PAST RESULTS 01
Sales management system

It is a system for creating quotations, processing orders, managing purchases and shipments or production, issuing invoices, and managing accounts receivable and sales.

The format of the master, quotation, invoice, etc. is reflected according to the product handled by the customer, so the introduction of the system does not significantly change the work, but it does not change the past performance survey and application, but also for each period, department or worker You will be able to instantly aggregate sales and other information.


PAST RESULTS 02
Production management system

It is a system that manages materials such as first-in first-out and surplus, clarifies the production process, manages plans and results, and manages products after production.

This eliminates inventory gaps and surpluses, reduces inspection labor, and prevents out-of-stocks so that you do not miss out on sales opportunities, thereby securing cash and stabilizing cash flow.


PAST RESULTS 03
Inventory management system

It is a system that manages the stock stored in the warehouse etc. Keeps the appropriate quantity.

Handy terminals and IC tags are often used, preventing out of stock and eliminating the need to search for inventory, which not only improves business efficiency but also stabilizes sales.


PAST RESULTS 04
Attendance management system

It is a system that records the time of arrival and leaving the office, records the break time and outgoing times, and manages work during working hours.

The use of IC tags when entering and leaving the company, the use of smartphone GPS to automate these records, the calculation of salaries using time and attendance records, the confirmation of work progress, etc. Useful for working style reforms such as reducing overtime.


PAST RESULTS 05
Order management system

An online shop is set up on the homepage to display product stocks and prices, to accept orders and make online payments.

It will be possible to accept orders 24 hours a day, which will not only increase sales chances, but also make payments at the same time as orders, eliminating the hassle and omission of operations and smoothing the flow to shipping.


PAST RESULTS 06
Insurance claim system

This system calculates insurance claims generated in hospitals and welfare facilities, manages users, creates medical records, and reports on service implementation.

The system flexibly responds to the contents and revisions of various insurances, and not only does the system perform complicated calculations instead, but it also automatically prepares documents that need to be submitted to the government, etc., using ordinary services and create charts. PAST RESULTS 07
Photo management system

It is a system that organizes the necessary photos at the stage of taking work, not only makes it easier to find later, but also automatically uses it for reports etc.

Even after taking a large number of photos, you can easily find out what kind of photo it is, so you can reduce the trouble of searching again and mistakes in use, and use the cloud to instantly share photos with people at remote locations, And the photos used for monthly and yearly reports are automatically generated.
